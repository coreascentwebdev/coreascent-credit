Frequently Asked Questions

Will applying affect my credit score?
Checking your options involves a soft credit inquiry, which does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs if you accept an offer and proceed with financing.
How quickly will I know if I'm approved?
Most applicants receive a decision within minutes. In some cases, additional verification may take up to 24 hours.
What credit score do I need?
We work with multiple lending partners to serve a range of credit profiles. Even if your credit isn't perfect, you may still have options available to you.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. All terms, rates, and fees are clearly disclosed before you accept any offer. You'll know exactly what your monthly payment will be upfront.
Can I pay off my balance early?
Yes. There are no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your balance at any time without additional charges.
What providers accept this financing?
Our financing is accepted at participating providers across dental, medical, veterinary, auto repair, and other service categories. Ask your provider if they work with Core Ascent, or contact us for details.
